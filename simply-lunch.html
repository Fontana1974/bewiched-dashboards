Priority Action Plan
🔴 URGENT · Higham Ferrers SL wastage at 1.39% of sales — highest rate in the estate. Site is wasting £272/month on a £19.6k turnover. Review delivery par levels; consider moving from 3× to 2× weekly delivery with smaller drops. Target: bring under 0.7%.

🔴 URGENT · Rushden Lakes Highest absolute waste at £691 (431 units). 0.86% of sales. Given the high volume, investigate daily sell-through data — are there specific days/SKUs driving this?
🟡 REVIEW · Fletton Quays SL spend was 16% of sales in June 2025 — a major outlier vs 9% network average. June 2026 spend data needed (P&L not available yet) but watch this closely. Site has strong salad and wrap sales which are higher-cost SKUs.
🟡 REVIEW · Tuna Crunch & Salad Range Tuna Crunch Sandwich should be either properly ranged across all sites or removed. Greek Chicken and Green & Grain Salads are low-velocity items at risk of contributing to wastage — review JJ Latimer data on spoilage by SKU.
🟢 OPPORTUNITY · Yoghurt Granola Pot Selling well where ranged (465 units, ~£1,500 revenue). Missing from at least 6 sites. Ranging consistently across estate could add ~£8-10k annual revenue at strong margin (cost £1.45 → retail £3.30).
🟢 OPPORTUNITY · Sausage Roll Format (DT) Northampton DT selling 386 units of sausage roll vs Billing at 158. Is this a display / positioning issue at Billing and Glenvale? Replicate the Northampton DT merchandising approach.
